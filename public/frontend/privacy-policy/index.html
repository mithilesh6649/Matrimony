The User Information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the Sites or APPs or WAPs, the choices you make and the products and features you use. The User Information we collect can include the following:
i. Personal Information. We collect personal data that you provide while registering on our platform such as name, email address, education, occupation, Country, etc.

All User Information that you provide to us must be true, complete and accurate, and you must notify us of any changes to such User Information. By choosing to provide this personal data, you consent to our processing of that data.
Information automatically collected
When you use our Sites or APPs or WAPs, we automatically collect and store certain information about your computer or mobile device and your activities.
This information includes:
- Mobile Device ID. Your mobile device's unique ID number.
- Geographic Location. Your mobile device's geographic location.
- Device Information/Specifications. We use this to offer you services such as authenticating you, remembering your preferences and settings, analysing site traffic and trends, delivering and measuring the effectiveness of advertising campaigns, allowing you to use social features and how you can better control their use, through your browser settings and other tools.
- Log Files. This may include IP addresses, browser type, ISP referring/exit pages, operating system information.

Web beacons (also known as "clear gifs" and "pixel tags"), are small, transparent graphic images that are often used in conjunction with cookies in order to further personalize our services for our users, collect a limited set of information about our visitors. We may also use web beacons in email communications in order to understand the behaviour of our customers, such as whether an email has been opened or acted upon.
